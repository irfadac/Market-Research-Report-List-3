<p><p>Veneer dentistry encompasses two main types of materials used in the market: composite and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made of a tooth-colored resin material that is directly bonded to the tooth. They are more affordable and can be placed in a single visit. Dental porcelain veneers, on the other hand, are thin shells made of porcelain that are custom-made in a dental laboratory and bonded to the tooth. They are more durable and have a more natural appearance, but they are also more expensive.</p></p>
