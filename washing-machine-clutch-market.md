<p><ul><li>Wave Type</li><li>Drum Type</li><li>Stirring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Washing machine clutches come in three main types: wave type, drum type, and stirring type. Wave type clutches use a wave spring design for smooth and efficient operation. Drum type clutches utilize a drum shape to engage and disengage the clutch. Stirring type clutches have a stirring mechanism that helps in evenly distributing the load. Each type of clutch has its own advantages and is suited for specific applications depending on the washing machine model and brand.</p></p>
