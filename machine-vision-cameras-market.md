<p><strong>The Machine Vision Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision cameras are broadly classified into two types, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer high image quality and sensitivity, making them ideal for applications requiring precise imaging with low noise levels. On the other hand, CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption and cost-effectiveness, making them a popular choice for high-speed imaging applications. Both types of cameras have their own set of advantages and are widely used in various industries for machine vision applications.</p></p>
