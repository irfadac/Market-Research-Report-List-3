<p><p>Chemical Vapour Deposition Device market includes two primary types - Catalytic CVD and Metal ALD. Catalytic CVD involves the use of a catalyst to initiate the chemical reaction between gas precursors, resulting in a thin film deposition on the substrate. Metal ALD, on the other hand, utilizes alternating pulses of metal precursors and reactants to achieve precise control over film thickness and composition. Both technologies are crucial in the semiconductor, optics, and coatings industries for the production of advanced materials with specific properties.</p></p>
