<p><p>Fiber coupled laser diodes come in two main types: Single mode and Multimode. Single mode laser diodes are designed to emit a single beam of light in a tightly focused manner, allowing for high precision applications. Multimode laser diodes emit multiple beams of light with varying wavelengths, making them suitable for applications where a broader range of wavelengths is needed. Both types of laser diodes are widely used in various industries such as telecommunications, medical devices, and industrial manufacturing for their unique capabilities and performance characteristics.</p></p>
