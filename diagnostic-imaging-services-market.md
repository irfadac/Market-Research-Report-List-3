<p><p>Diagnostic imaging services market encompasses various types including diagnostic radiology, ultrasound, computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), nuclear imaging, and others. Diagnostic radiology utilizes various imaging techniques to diagnose and treat diseases. Ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves to create images of structures inside the body. CT scans combine X-rays and computer technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images. MRI uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of organs and tissues. Nuclear imaging involves the use of small amounts of radioactive materials to diagnose and treat a variety of conditions. Other imaging services may include PET scans, mammography, and fluoroscopy.</p></p>
