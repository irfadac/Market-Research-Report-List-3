<p><strong>The Chip Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dielectric Chip Antennas</li><li>LTCC Chip Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip antennas are small antennas built onto a substrate, typically ceramic or laminate. Dielectric chip antennas utilize the dielectric properties of the substrate material to achieve desired antenna characteristics. LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic) chip antennas are built using specialized ceramic materials that can withstand high temperatures during manufacturing. These antennas offer high performance and compact size, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. Both types of chip antennas are widely used in wireless communication devices.</p></p>
