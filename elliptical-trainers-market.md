<p><p>Elliptical trainers come in three different market types: Front Drive, Rear Drive, and Center Drive. Front Drive ellipticals have the flywheel located at the front of the machine, providing a smooth and natural elliptical motion. Rear Drive ellipticals have the flywheel located at the back, which helps simulate the feeling of walking or running. Center Drive ellipticals have the flywheel situated in the middle, offering a more compact design and a more balanced workout. Each type of elliptical trainer has its own unique benefits and features to suit different users' needs.</p></p>
