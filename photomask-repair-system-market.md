<p><ul><li>Laser Technology</li><li>Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology</li><li>Nanomachining Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask repair systems use various technologies for repairing defects on photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing. Laser technology involves using high-energy laser beams to remove unwanted material from the photomask. Focused Ion Beam (FIB) technology uses a beam of ions to accurately remove or deposit material on the mask. Nanomachining technology employs extremely precise tools to manipulate materials at the nanoscale level. Each of these technologies offers unique benefits and capabilities for efficiently repairing photomasks in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
