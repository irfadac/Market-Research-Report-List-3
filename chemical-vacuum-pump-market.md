Liquid ring vacuum pumps use a liquid as a sealant to create the vacuum, suitable for handling corrosive gases. Rotary vane vacuum pumps use rotating vanes to create the vacuum, ideal for high vacuum applications. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different industrial applications.</p></p>
