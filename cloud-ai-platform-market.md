<p><p>Cloud AI Platform Market can be categorized into Public Clouds, Private Clouds, and Hybrid Clouds based on deployment types. Public Clouds are hosted and managed by third-party providers, offering on-demand computing resources to multiple users. Private Clouds are dedicated environments for a single organization, providing more control and customization. Hybrid Clouds combine both public and private cloud infrastructure, allowing businesses to balance security and scalability. Each type offers different levels of security, cost-effectiveness, and flexibility to cater to varying business needs.</p></p>
