<p><ul><li>Resettable Fuse Resistor</li><li>One-time Fuse Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fusing resistor market includes resettable fuse resistors and one-time fuse resistors. Resettable fuse resistors are designed to automatically reset after a fault condition is removed, making them ideal for applications where continuous protection is needed. One-time fuse resistors, on the other hand, need to be replaced after a fault occurs, providing a one-time protective solution. Both types of fusing resistors are used in various electronic devices and applications to prevent damage from overcurrent or short circuit events.</p></p>
