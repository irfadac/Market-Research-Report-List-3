<p><p>Cell invasion and migration assays are used to study cell movement and invasion in various biological processes. Colorimetry assay measures the color change caused by cells migrating or invading through a membrane, providing a quantifiable measurement of cell movement. Fluorescence assay utilizes fluorescent dyes to track and visualize cell migration and invasion in real-time. Both types of assays are essential in understanding cellular behavior and are widely used in research, drug development, and disease mechanisms studies.</p></p>
