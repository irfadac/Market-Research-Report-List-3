<p><p>Polycrystalline Alumina Fiber Market consists of two main types - Mullite Fiber and High Alumina Fiber. Mullite fiber is a type of crystalline fiber made from a mixture of alumina and silica, known for its high temperature resistance and low thermal conductivity. High Alumina Fiber, on the other hand, is made predominantly from alumina, offering excellent thermal stability and high strength at elevated temperatures. Both types of fibers are used in various industries including aerospace, automotive, and electronics for their exceptional properties.</p></p>
