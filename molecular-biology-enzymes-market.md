<p><p>Molecular Biology Enzymes market is divided into types such as polymerases, ligases, restriction enzymes, and other enzymes. Polymerases are enzymes that catalyze the synthesis of new DNA strands. Ligases are responsible for joining DNA fragments together. Restriction enzymes cleave DNA at specific sequences, aiding in genetic engineering. Other enzymes in this market include helicases, topoisomerases, and exonucleases, which play various roles in DNA replication, repair, and modification processes. These enzymes are crucial for molecular biology research and applications.</p></p>
