<p><p>Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) Insurance is a type of coverage that protects borrowers from financial loss in the event that their vehicle is declared a total loss by their insurance company and the amount received is lower than the amount owed on the car loan. GAP insurance covers the "gap" between the car's actual cash value and the amount still owed on the loan, ensuring that the borrower does not have to pay out of pocket to settle the remaining balance.</p><p>The Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) Insurance Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.
