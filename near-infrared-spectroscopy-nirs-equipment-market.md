<p><p>Near-infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) Equipment Market includes three main types: FT-NIR Spectroscopy, Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy, and Other (AOTF, Filter). FT-NIR Spectroscopy utilizes Fourier transform technology for high-resolution measurements, while Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy uses diffraction gratings to disperse light into various wavelengths. The Other category includes equipment utilizing technologies such as Acousto-Optic Tunable Filter (AOTF) and filters. Each type of NIRS equipment offers unique benefits and capabilities for applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food processing.</p></p>
