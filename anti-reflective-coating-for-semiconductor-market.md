<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Anti-reflective Coating for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Anti-reflective Coating(BARC)</li><li>Top Anti-reflective Coating(TARC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-reflective coatings are used in semiconductors to reduce reflection and improve light transmission. Bottom Anti-reflective Coating (BARC) is applied underneath the photoresist layer to reduce interference from reflections bouncing off the substrate. Top Anti-reflective Coating (TARC) is applied on top of the photoresist to minimize reflections at the resist-air interface. Both types of coatings help enhance lithography performance by ensuring more precise patterning and better photoresist adhesion.</p></p>
