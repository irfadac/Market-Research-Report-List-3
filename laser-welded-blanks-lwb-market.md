<p><p>Laser Welded Blanks (LWB) are sheets of metal that are welded together using laser technology. There are three main types of LWB markets: linear welded blanks, non-linear welded blanks, and curve-linear welded blanks. Linear welded blanks are straight welds used in applications such as automotive body panels. Non-linear welded blanks involve complex shapes and geometries, while curve-linear welded blanks are used for parts with curved or bent profiles. Each type of LWB market serves different industries and applications based on their specific welding requirements.</p></p>
