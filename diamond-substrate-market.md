<p><p>Diamond substrate market is segmented into two main types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) Substrate and High Pressure High Temperature (HPHT) Diamond Substrate. CVD substrate is produced through a chemical process involving the deposition of diamond on a substrate material, while HPHT substrate is created through high pressure and high temperature conditions to mimic natural diamond formation. Both types of diamond substrates are used in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive for their superior hardness, thermal conductivity, and optical properties.</p></p>
