<p><p>Fireproof valve market types include the normally closed exhaust fire damper, smoke exhaust valve, automatic smoke fire valve, and other variations. The normally closed exhaust fire damper is designed to prevent the spread of fire by sealing off ductwork. The smoke exhaust valve helps to control the movement of smoke in the event of a fire. Automatic smoke fire valves activate in response to smoke detection to contain and redirect smoke. Other types of fireproof valves include variations that serve specific functions in fire protection systems.</p></p>
