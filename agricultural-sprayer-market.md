<p><p>Agricultural sprayers come in various types to suit different farming needs. Handheld sprayers are portable and used for small-scale applications. Self-propelled sprayers are larger and suitable for medium to large farms. Tractor-mounted sprayers are attached to tractors for easy application. Trailed sprayers are towed behind tractors and offer a larger capacity for spraying. Aerial sprayers are used for large-scale operations and can cover vast areas efficiently from the air. Each type of sprayer offers unique benefits depending on the size and requirements of the farm.</p></p>
