<p><p>Rapid Thermal Annealers are commonly classified into lamp-based and laser-based types in the market. Lamp-based annealers use intense light emitted from a lamp to heat the substrate quickly and uniformly. On the other hand, laser-based annealers employ laser beams to achieve rapid and precise heating of the substrate. Both types are widely used in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing for processes like annealing, cleaning, and oxidation due to their ability to provide high temperatures in a short amount of time.</p></p>
