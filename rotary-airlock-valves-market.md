<p><p>Rotary airlock valves are used to control the flow of materials in various industries. There are two main types of rotary airlock valves: drop-through rotary valves and blow-through rotary valves. Drop-through rotary valves are designed for handling free-flowing materials and are ideal for gravity-fed applications. On the other hand, blow-through rotary valves are used for handling powdery or granular materials and are suitable for pneumatic conveying systems. Both types of valves play a critical role in ensuring efficient and reliable material handling processes.</p></p>
