<p><p>HVAC packaged units come in two main types based on the condenser used - air condenser and water condenser. Air condenser units use outdoor air to remove heat from the refrigerant, while water condenser units use water as a cooling medium. Air condenser units are more commonly used in residential and small commercial buildings, while water condenser units are typically found in larger commercial and industrial applications. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as space availability, efficiency, and cost.</p></p>
