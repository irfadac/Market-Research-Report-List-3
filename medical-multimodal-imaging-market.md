<p><p>Medical Multimodal Imaging Market types include PET-CT, which combines positron emission tomography (PET) with computed tomography (CT) for more precise imaging of tumors and other abnormalities. SPECT-CT merges single-photon emission computed tomography (SPECT) with CT to enhance diagnostic accuracy. PET-MR combines PET with magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) for comprehensive imaging of organs and tissues. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) utilizes light waves to create high-resolution images of tissues. Other markets in this sector include hybrid imaging modalities and advanced imaging techniques for better diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
