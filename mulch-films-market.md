<p><p>Mulch films are used in agriculture to cover the soil and improve crop growth. They can be categorized into three main types based on the material used: LLDPE, LDPE, and HDPE. LLDPE mulch films are lightweight and offer good strength and puncture resistance. LDPE mulch films are more flexible and provide better moisture retention. HDPE mulch films are durable and offer excellent UV protection. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different agricultural needs and preferences.</p></p>
