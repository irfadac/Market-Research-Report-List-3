<p><p>Demand Side Platform (DSP) System Market Types includes RTB (Real Time Bidding) and PPB (Programmatic Premium Buying). RTB involves buying ad impressions in real time through automated auctions, allowing advertisers to bid only on the most relevant audiences. PPB, on the other hand, focuses on purchasing high-quality ad inventory directly from publishers at fixed rates. While RTB is more cost-effective, PPB offers better control over where ads are placed. Both market types play a crucial role in programmatic advertising strategies.</p></p>
