<p><p>Medical temperature management market is divided into two main types: patient cooling systems and patient warming systems. </p><p>Patient cooling systems are used to lower the body temperature of critically ill patients, typically those undergoing cardiac arrest or experiencing high fevers. This process helps reduce the risk of neurological damage and improve patient outcomes. </p><p>On the other hand, patient warming systems are designed to maintain or increase the body temperature of patients during surgery or recovery, preventing hypothermia and promoting faster healing. These two types of systems play a crucial role in regulating patient temperature for optimal health and recovery.</p></p>
