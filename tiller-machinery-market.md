<p><p>The tiller machinery market consists of two main types: wheeled type and crawler type. Wheeled type tillers are equipped with wheels, providing greater mobility and maneuverability on even terrain. Crawler type tillers, on the other hand, have tracks like a tank, offering better traction and stability on rough or sloped surfaces. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of farmers and agricultural workers, with wheeled type being more suitable for flat fields and crawler type better for challenging terrains.</p></p>
