<p><p>LTE IoT refers to two types of technologies: LTE-M (LTE-Machine) and NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT). LTE-M is designed for applications that require higher data rates and mobility, making it suitable for devices like wearables and asset trackers. NB-IoT, on the other hand, is optimized for low power consumption and extended coverage, making it ideal for use cases such as smart meters and agriculture sensors. Both technologies cater to different market needs, providing a diverse range of solutions for IoT deployments.</p></p>
