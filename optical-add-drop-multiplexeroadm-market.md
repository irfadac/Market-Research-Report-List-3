<p><p>Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers (OADM) are devices used in optical networks to add or drop specific wavelengths of light signals. There are two main types of OADM markets: Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers, which have fixed configurations and cannot be reconfigured, and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers, which offer flexibility to dynamically add or drop wavelengths as needed. Fixed OADMs are cost-effective for static networks, while reconfigurable OADMs are suitable for dynamic and evolving network environments.</p></p>
