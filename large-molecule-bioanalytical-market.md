<p><p>The Large Molecule Bioanalytical market encompasses various types of bioanalytical services, including ADME (Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, and Excretion), PK (Pharmacokinetics), and PD (Pharmacodynamics) studies. ADME studies focus on understanding how the body processes large molecules, while PK studies evaluate the movement of these molecules throughout the body. PD studies, on the other hand, examine the biological effects of large molecules. These types of bioanalytical services play a crucial role in drug development and ensuring the safety and efficacy of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
