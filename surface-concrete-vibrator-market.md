<p><p>Surface concrete vibrators are categorized based on their frequency of vibration: High frequency vibrators operate at speeds between 10,000 to 20,000 vibrations per minute, providing a smooth finish for shallow concrete pours. Medium frequency vibrators operate at speeds around 5,000 to 10,000 vibrations per minute, suitable for medium-depth concrete pours. Low frequency vibrators operate at speeds below 2,000 vibrations per minute, ideal for deep or thick concrete pours. Each type caters to specific concrete pouring needs, offering different levels of efficiency and finishing quality.</p></p>
