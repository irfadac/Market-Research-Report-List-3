<p><strong>The Thermal Sprayed Aluminum Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flame Spraying</li><li>Arc Spraying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Sprayed Aluminum Coatings market includes two main types - Flame Spraying and Arc Spraying. Flame Spraying involves heating the aluminum powder in a flame and projecting it onto the surface to form a coating. This method is commonly used for smaller, intricate parts. Arc Spraying, on the other hand, involves melting the aluminum wire in an electric arc and spraying it onto the surface. This method is often used for larger, more robust applications due to its higher deposition rates.</p></p>
