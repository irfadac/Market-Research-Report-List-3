<p><p>Hair removal market can be categorized into three main types: energy-based devices, laser-based devices, and IPL (intense pulsed light) devices. Energy-based devices use heat or light energy to target hair follicles, inhibiting hair growth. Laser-based devices emit specific wavelengths of light to target melanin in hair follicles, resulting in hair removal. IPL devices also use light energy to damage hair follicles and disrupt hair growth. These technologies offer efficient and long-lasting hair removal solutions for consumers seeking a convenient and effective method.</p></p>
