<p><strong>The Digital Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital mass flow controllers are used in various industries to accurately control the flow of gases. The two main types in the market are thermal pressure type and differential pressure type. </p><p>Thermal pressure type controllers use a sensing element that measures the heat transfer from a heated coil to the gas flow, providing accurate and fast control. </p><p>On the other hand, differential pressure type controllers rely on a pressure drop measurement across a fixed orifice to determine the flow rate, making them suitable for applications where high accuracy is required.</p></p>
