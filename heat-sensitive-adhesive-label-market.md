<p><p>Heat sensitive adhesive labels come in two main types: non-reversible and reversible. Non-reversible labels adhere permanently once exposed to heat, making them ideal for long-term applications. Reversible labels, on the other hand, can be removed and reapplied multiple times without leaving residue behind. Both types cater to different market needs, with non-reversible labels being preferred for durable and permanent labeling, while reversible labels are more suitable for temporary or interchangeable applications.</p></p>
