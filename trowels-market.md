<p><p>Walk-behind trowels are compact and maneuverable machines that are ideal for finishing smaller areas of concrete. They are commonly used for residential projects or in tight spaces where larger equipment cannot reach. On the other hand, ride-on trowels are larger machines that are better suited for larger scale projects such as commercial buildings, warehouses, or industrial floors. They are designed to increase productivity and reduce operator fatigue by allowing the operator to ride on the machine while finishing the concrete surface.</p></p>
