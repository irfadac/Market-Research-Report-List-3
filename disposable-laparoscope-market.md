<p><p>Disposable laparoscope market includes various types of instruments such as bipolar instruments, monopolar instruments, trocars, graspers, and dissectors. Bipolar instruments are used for precise coagulation and cutting, while monopolar instruments are used for cutting and dissecting tissue. Trocars are used to create initial access to the abdominal cavity, graspers are used to hold and manipulate tissue, and dissectors are used to separate tissue layers. These instruments play a crucial role in laparoscopic surgeries by facilitating various surgical procedures with precision and efficiency.</p></p>
