<p><p>PTZ security cameras can be categorized into two main market types: centralized cameras and decentralized cameras. Centralized cameras are controlled and managed by a central monitoring station, providing a more traditional approach to surveillance. On the other hand, decentralized cameras operate independently, with each camera having its built-in recorder, storage, and processing capabilities. Decentralized cameras offer greater flexibility and scalability as they do not rely on a central server. Both types cater to different surveillance needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
