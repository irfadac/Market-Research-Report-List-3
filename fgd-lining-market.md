<p><strong>The FGD Lining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Vulcanized Lining</li><li>Cold Vulcanized Lining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FGD lining market includes two main types: hot vulcanized lining and cold vulcanized lining. Hot vulcanized lining involves the process of bonding rubber sheets using heat and pressure, resulting in a durable and seamless lining. Cold vulcanized lining, on the other hand, involves using adhesives to bond rubber sheets, which is a quicker and more cost-effective option. Both types of linings are used in industries such as power plants, chemical processing, and mining to protect equipment from corrosion and abrasion.</p></p>
