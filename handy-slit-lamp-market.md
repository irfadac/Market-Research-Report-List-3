<p><p>Handy slit lamps are essential equipment in ophthalmology for examining the anterior segment of the eye. There are two types of market segments for handy slit lamps: monocular and binocular. Monocular slit lamps have a single eyepiece, making them more compact and affordable, making them suitable for basic eye examinations. On the other hand, binocular slit lamps have two eyepieces, providing a more comfortable and detailed viewing experience, making them ideal for more advanced examinations and procedures. Both types cater to different needs and budgets in the ophthalmology market.</p></p>
