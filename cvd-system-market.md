<p><strong>The CVD System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal CVD</li><li>PECVD</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CVD system market is categorized into three main types: Normal CVD, PECVD, and Others. Normal CVD refers to traditional chemical vapor deposition processes, where a chemical reaction occurs in the gas phase to deposit a thin film on a substrate. PECVD stands for plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition, which uses a plasma to enhance the chemical reactions and produce high-quality films at lower temperatures. The "Others" category includes other variations and specialized types of CVD systems used in specific industries or applications.</p></p>
