<p><p>Electric crawler excavators are available in two main types: backhoe excavators and front shovel excavators. Backhoe excavators have a digging bucket attached to the back of the machine, making them ideal for excavation projects where the depth is limited. On the other hand, front shovel excavators have a digging bucket attached to the front of the machine, making them suitable for projects that require high digging force and increased efficiency. Both types of excavators are commonly used in construction, mining, and other heavy-duty industries.</p></p>
