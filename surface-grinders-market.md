<p><p>Planer type surface grinders are machines with a stationary grinding wheel and a reciprocating table. They are ideal for producing large flat surfaces on workpieces. On the other hand, rotary type surface grinders have a rotating grinding wheel and a stationary workpiece. These machines are suitable for achieving high precision and smooth finishes on cylindrical or non-flat surfaces. Both types of surface grinders can have a wide range of applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
