<p><p>Premature Rupture of Membranes (PROM) testing market includes various types of tests such as Nitrazine/pH Test, Ferning Test, Amnisure Test, and others. Nitrazine/pH test detects the pH level of vaginal fluid, which changes when amniotic fluid is present. Ferning test examines the dried vaginal fluid under a microscope for fern-like patterns that indicate amniotic fluid. Amnisure test detects a protein specific to amniotic fluid in vaginal discharge. Other tests may include ultrasound imaging or ultrasound detection of amniotic fluid.</p></p>
