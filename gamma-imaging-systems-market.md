<p><p>Gamma imaging systems are classified into two main market types: measurement type and detection type. Measurement type systems are used to measure the activity of a specific radioactive material in a sample, providing quantitative data on the amount of radiation present. Detection type systems, on the other hand, are used to simply detect the presence of radiation without quantifying the amount. Both types of systems play a crucial role in various industries such as healthcare, research, and security, ensuring safe handling of radioactive materials and accurate monitoring of radiation levels.</p></p>
