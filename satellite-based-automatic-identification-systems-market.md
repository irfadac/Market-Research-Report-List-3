<p><p>Satellite-based Automatic Identification Systems (AIS) include two main market types: Class A Transponder and Class B Transponder. Class A transponders are typically used by larger vessels and are required to transmit data such as vessel identity, position, speed, and course. Class B transponders, on the other hand, are usually used by smaller vessels and transmit less data than Class A transponders. Both types of transponders help improve maritime safety and efficiency by allowing vessels to easily track and communicate with each other.</p></p>
