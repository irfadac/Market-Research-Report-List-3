<p><strong>The Depth Filtration Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration)</li><li>Bottom-In–Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Depth filtration devices are commonly used in two different configurations in the market. The Bottom-In–Top-Out mode, also known as the inline configuration, involves feeding the fluid at the bottom of the device and collecting the filtrate at the top. On the other hand, the Bottom-In–Bottom-Out mode, which is also referred to as T-configuration, requires the fluid to enter and exit the device at the bottom. These different configurations cater to diverse filtration needs and preferences of users in various industries.</p></p>
