Daily work clothing is worn by healthcare professionals on a day-to-day basis and includes scrubs, lab coats, and jackets. Special protective clothing is designed to protect healthcare workers from hazardous materials or infections, such as gowns, coveralls, and aprons. Each type caters to different needs within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
