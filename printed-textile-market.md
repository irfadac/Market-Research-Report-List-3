<p><p>Printed textile market can be classified into three main types: Natural textile, Synthetic textile, and Combined textile. Natural textiles are made from materials obtained from plants, animals, or minerals, such as cotton, silk, and wool. Synthetic textiles are man-made materials like polyester and nylon. Combined textiles are a blend of both natural and synthetic fibers, offering the benefits of both. Each type of textile market caters to different consumer preferences, with natural textiles being preferred for their comfort and sustainability, while synthetic textiles are valued for their durability and affordability.</p></p>
