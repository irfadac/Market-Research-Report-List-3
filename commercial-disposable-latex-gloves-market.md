Both types serve the same purpose of providing a barrier between the user's hands and potentially harmful materials. Powdered gloves are preferred by some users for their ease of use, while non-powdered gloves are favored by those who are sensitive to the powder or working with certain substances that must remain uncontaminated.</p></p>
