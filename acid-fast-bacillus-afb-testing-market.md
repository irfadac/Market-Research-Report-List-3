<p><p>Acid-Fast Bacillus (AFB) Testing is a laboratory test used to diagnose diseases caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis and other acid-fast bacteria. The test involves staining a sample with a special dye that binds to the high lipid content of the bacterial cell walls, resulting in a distinctive red color under a microscope. A positive test indicates the presence of acid-fast bacilli in the sample, which is indicative of diseases like tuberculosis.</p><p>The Acid-Fast Bacillus (AFB) Testing Market is witnessing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.
