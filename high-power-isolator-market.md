<p><strong>The High Power Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Dual-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-stage high power isolators are commonly used in applications where there is a need for high isolation levels in a single optical path. They are designed to provide high isolation between the input and output ports. On the other hand, dual-stage high power isolators consist of two isolating stages connected in series, offering even higher levels of isolation. They are typically used in more demanding optical systems where enhanced isolation performance is required to prevent signal degradation or interference.</p></p>
