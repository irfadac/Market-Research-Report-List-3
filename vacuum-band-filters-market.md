Horizontal filters, on the other hand, have the filter cloth moving horizontally, enabling effective separation of solids and liquids. Both types of filters are commonly used in industries such as mining, chemical processing, and wastewater treatment to separate solids from liquids efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
