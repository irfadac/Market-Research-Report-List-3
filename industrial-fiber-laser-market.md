<p><p>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser is a type of industrial fiber laser that emits a continuous beam of laser light. This type of laser is ideal for applications requiring steady and consistent laser output. </p><p>Pulsed Fiber Laser, on the other hand, emits laser pulses in short bursts. This type of laser is commonly used for applications that require high peak power but do not need a continuous output. Pulsed Fiber Lasers are suitable for marking, engraving, cutting, and welding applications in various industries.</p></p>
