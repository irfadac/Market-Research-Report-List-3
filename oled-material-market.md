<p><p>OLED material market includes various types such as Hole Transport Layer (HTL), Electron Transport Layer (ETL), Hole Blocking Layer (HBL), Hole Injection Layer (HIL), Encapsulation materials, Emissive Layer (EML), Substrate materials, and Anode materials. HTL, ETL, HBL, and HIL are used for transporting charges in OLED devices. Encapsulation materials protect OLED components from external factors. EML emits light when electricity is applied. Substrate materials provide a base for OLED layers. Anode materials help in the flow of electrons. These materials play crucial roles in the performance and durability of OLED displays.</p></p>
