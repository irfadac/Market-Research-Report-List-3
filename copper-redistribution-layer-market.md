<p><p>Copper Redistribution Layer (Cu RDL) and Copper/Nickel/Gold Redistribution Layer (Cu/Ni/Au RDL) are two main types of metal layers used in semiconductor packaging. Cu RDL is mainly used for low-cost applications due to its electrical conductivity and good adhesion properties. On the other hand, Cu/Ni/Au RDL is preferred for high-reliability applications due to its enhanced corrosion resistance and solderability. Both types of RDLs play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and reliability of advanced semiconductor devices.</p></p>
