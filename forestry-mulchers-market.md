<p><p>Forestry mulchers market is segmented into two types based on how they are attached to the vehicle: Mounted Mulchers and Offset Mulchers. Mounted Mulchers are directly attached to the front or rear of a vehicle, providing efficient mulching capabilities close to the machine. Offset Mulchers, on the other hand, are attached to the side of the vehicle, allowing for mulching in hard-to-reach areas such as along fences or road edges. Both types offer effective solutions for land clearing and vegetation management in forestry and agriculture industries.</p></p>
