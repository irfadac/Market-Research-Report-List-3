<p><ul><li>Latex-bonded Airlaid Napkin</li><li>Thermal-bonded Airlaid Napkin</li><li>Multi-bonded Airlaid Napkin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airlaid Napkins market consists of three main types: Latex-bonded, Thermal-bonded, and Multi-bonded Airlaid Napkins. Latex-bonded napkins are made by bonding fibers together with a latex binder, resulting in a soft and absorbent product. Thermal-bonded napkins are created using heat and pressure to bond fibers together, offering a strong and durable option. Multi-bonded napkins combine different bonding methods to achieve specific properties such as strength and absorbency. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
