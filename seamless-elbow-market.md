<p><p>Seamless elbows are essential components in various industries such as oil and gas, construction, and automotive. There are two main types of seamless elbows in the market: hot rolled seamless elbows and cold drawn seamless elbows. Hot rolled seamless elbows are manufactured by heating a solid billet and shaping it into a seamless elbow, while cold drawn seamless elbows are made by pulling a solid tube through a die to achieve the desired shape. Both types offer high-quality, durable, and precise fittings for piping systems.</p></p>
