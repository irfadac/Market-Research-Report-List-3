<p><p>Cancer Tissue Diagnostics market encompasses various types of diagnostic tests, including Immunohistochemistry (IHC) Test and In Situ Hybridisation Test. IHC Test involves the detection of antigens in tissue samples using specific antibodies, helping in understanding the cellular composition of cancer cells. On the other hand, In Situ Hybridisation Test assists in detecting specific DNA or RNA sequences within tissue samples, aiding in the identification of genetic abnormalities. Both tests play a crucial role in accurately diagnosing and treating cancer patients.</p></p>
