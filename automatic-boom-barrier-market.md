<p><p>The automatic boom barrier market offers three main types of control mechanisms: remote control, RFID tags/reader, and loop detectors. Remote control systems allow users to operate the barrier from a distance. RFID tags and readers use radio frequency identification technology to grant access to authorized vehicles. Loop detectors use sensors embedded in the ground to detect vehicles and trigger the barrier to open or close. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications in controlling vehicle access.</p></p>
