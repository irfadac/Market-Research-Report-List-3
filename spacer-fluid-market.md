<p><strong>The Spacer Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscous Spacer</li><li>Turbulent Spacer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spacer Fluid Market consists of two main types, Viscous Spacer and Turbulent Spacer. Viscous Spacer fluids are designed to create a stable and controlled environment between two fluids, preventing them from mixing. They are typically used in applications where a clear separation between fluids is required. On the other hand, Turbulent Spacer fluids are designed to create turbulence and promote mixing between fluids. They are commonly used in applications where thorough mixing and dispersion of fluids is necessary. Both types cater to different market needs within the Spacer Fluid industry.</p></p>
