<p><ul><li>Hypothermic Machine Perfusion</li><li>Normothermic Machine Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical Perfusion Technology Market Types include Hypothermic Machine Perfusion and Normothermic Machine Perfusion. Hypothermic Machine Perfusion involves the continuous perfusion of an organ with a cold solution to preserve it for transplantation. This method is primarily used for kidneys and livers. Normothermic Machine Perfusion, on the other hand, involves perfusing the organ with warm, oxygenated blood at normal body temperature to assess its viability for transplantation. This method is being increasingly explored for various organs including hearts and lungs.</p></p>
