<p><p>Physical filtration involves removing debris and waste particles from the water using a physical barrier like a sponge or filter pad. Chemical filtration utilizes activated carbon or other chemical media to remove harmful substances like ammonia and nitrites from the water. Biological filtration relies on beneficial bacteria to break down waste into less harmful compounds. Each type of filtration plays a crucial role in maintaining a healthy and balanced ecosystem in fish ponds, ensuring clean and clear water for fish to thrive.</p></p>
