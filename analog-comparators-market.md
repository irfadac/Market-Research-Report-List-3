<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog comparators are available in two main market types: Surface Mount Device (SMD) and Through Hole. SMD comparators are smaller in size and are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. On the other hand, Through Hole comparators have leads that are soldered into holes on the PCB, providing a more secure mechanical connection. Both types of comparators offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of a project.</p></p>
