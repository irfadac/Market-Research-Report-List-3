The market for rail fastening systems can be categorized into different types, including rail spike series, track bolt series, rail clip series, and others. Rail spike series typically consist of spikes driven into wooden sleepers to secure rails. Track bolt series involve bolts used to secure rails to concrete sleepers. Rail clip series use clips to secure rails to baseplates. Other types of rail fastening systems may include clamps or pads for specific applications.</p></p>
