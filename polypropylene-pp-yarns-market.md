<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) yarns are categorized into two main types: Polypropylene Multifilament Yarn and Polypropylene Monofilament Yarn. Polypropylene Multifilament Yarn consists of multiple filaments twisted together, providing strength and durability, making it suitable for applications such as ropes, nets, and geotextiles. On the other hand, Polypropylene Monofilament Yarn is a single continuous filament with a smooth surface, ideal for applications requiring a softer touch such as furniture upholstery, apparel, and automotive interiors. Both types of PP yarns offer unique qualities catering to different market needs.</p></p>
