<p><p>Hydrocracking catalysts are essential in the refining of crude oil to produce valuable products like gasoline and diesel fuel. There are two main types of hydrocracking catalysts: Transition Metal Catalysts and Precious Metal Catalysts. Transition Metal Catalysts are composed of metals like nickel and cobalt, which are abundant and cost-effective. Precious Metal Catalysts, on the other hand, consist of metals like platinum and palladium, which are more rare and expensive but offer higher catalytic activity and selectivity. Both types play crucial roles in the hydrocracking process.</p></p>
