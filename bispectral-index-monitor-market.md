<p><p>The Bispectral Index Monitor is a medical device used to monitor the depth of anesthesia during surgery or sedation. It measures the patient's level of consciousness by analyzing the electroencephalogram (EEG) signals and providing a numerical value known as the Bispectral Index (BIS). This helps anesthesiologists to adjust the dosage of anesthesia drugs accordingly, leading to better patient outcomes and reducing the risk of awareness during surgery.</p><p>The Bispectral Index Monitor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.00% during the forecast period.
