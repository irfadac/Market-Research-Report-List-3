<p><p>Ytterbium-Doped Fiber Amplifiers (YDFA) are classified into two main types based on their operation mode: Pulsed and Continuous Wave (CW) amplifiers. Ytterbium-Doped Pulsed Fiber Amplifiers are designed to amplify short optical pulses, making them suitable for applications such as laser machining and telecommunications. On the other hand, Ytterbium-Doped Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Amplifiers provide continuous wave output, making them ideal for applications like high-power industrial lasers and optical communications systems. Both types of amplifiers offer efficient and high-power amplification capabilities for various optical systems.</p></p>
