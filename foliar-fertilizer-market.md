<p><p>Foliar fertilizers are categorized into different types based on their nutrient composition:</p><p>1. Nitrogenous fertilizers provide nitrogen to plants, promoting growth and green foliage.</p><p>2. Phosphatic fertilizers supply phosphorus for strong root development and flowering.</p><p>3. Potassic fertilizers contain potassium for overall plant health, disease resistance, and optimal fruit production.</p><p>4. Macronutrients like calcium, magnesium, and sulfur are essential for plant growth in larger quantities.</p><p>5. Micronutrients such as iron, zinc, and manganese are needed in smaller amounts for specific functions like photosynthesis and enzyme activation.</p></p>
