<p><p>Horizontal lathes are categorized into two main types based on the rail system: Horizontal Guide Rail Lathes and Inclined Rail Lathes. Horizontal Guide Rail Lathes have a horizontal guide rail system that helps in precise and stable movement of the cutting tool. On the other hand, Inclined Rail Lathes have an inclined rail system that offers enhanced rigidity and stability during the machining process. These different market types cater to the diverse needs of industries requiring high-precision and efficient turning operations.</p></p>
