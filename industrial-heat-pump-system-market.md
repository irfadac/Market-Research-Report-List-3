<p><p>Industrial Heat Pump Systems are categorized into various types based on their functionality. Air to Water Cylinder Heat Pumps extract heat from the air and transfer it to water for heating purposes. Groundwater Heat Pumps utilize the heat from underground water sources. Split air-to-water heat pumps consist of two separate units for heating and cooling. Exhaust Air Heat Pumps extract heat from the exhaust air and repurpose it for heating. Each type of heat pump system offers unique advantages and applications in industrial settings.</p></p>
