<p><p>A Metal Oxide Thin Film Transistor (TFT) is a type of thin film transistor technology that uses metal oxide semiconductors as the active layer. Metal oxides such as Zinc Oxide, Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide (IGZO), and Indium Oxide are commonly used in these transistors due to their high mobility and transparency, making them ideal for high-performance display devices such as LCDs and OLEDs.
