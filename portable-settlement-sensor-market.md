<p><p>Digital Settlement Sensors are portable devices that measure settlement or movement of the ground by converting the displacement into an electronic signal. Vibrating Wire Settlement Sensors use vibrating wire technology to detect settlement or movement in the ground. Both types are commonly used in construction, geotechnical engineering, and monitoring of infrastructure. Other types of portable settlement sensors include inclinometers, extensometers, and tiltmeters, which are also used for monitoring ground movement and settlements in various applications.</p></p>
