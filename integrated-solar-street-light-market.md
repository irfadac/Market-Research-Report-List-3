<p><p>Fully integrated solar street lights are all-in-one systems where the solar panels, LED lights, battery, and control system are all housed within a single unit. They are easy to install and require minimal maintenance. Semi-integrated solar street lights have the solar panels and battery separate from the light fixture, allowing for flexibility in installation and positioning. They are more customizable and offer the option to adjust the angle of the solar panels for optimal sunlight exposure. Both types of solar street lights are efficient and sustainable lighting solutions.</p></p>
