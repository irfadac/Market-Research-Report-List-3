<p><strong>The Photomask Review System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review System market can be categorized into two main types: Die to Die (DD) method and Die to Database (DB) method. The DD method involves comparing two separate photomasks to ensure alignment and accuracy. On the other hand, the DB method compares the photomask design data with the actual physical photomask. Both methods are essential in the semiconductor industry to ensure the quality and accuracy of photomasks used in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
