<p><p>Electrodeposition, also known as electroplating, is the process of using an electric current to deposit a thin layer of metal onto a conductive surface. This technique is widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, aerospace, and jewelry for coating surfaces with metals such as copper, nickel, gold, and silver. Electrodeposition offers advantages such as uniform coating thickness, improved corrosion resistance, and enhanced aesthetics.</p><p>The Electrodeposition Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
