<p><p>Motorized Spine Operating Tables are equipped with electric motors that allow for easy adjustment of table height, as well as tilting and lateral movement. These tables are ideal for surgeons who require precise positioning during complex spine surgeries. </p><p>Non-motorized Spine Operating Tables, on the other hand, rely on manual manipulation for adjustments. While they may be less expensive compared to motorized tables, they require more physical effort from surgical staff to position patients accurately. Both types of tables play a crucial role in providing a stable and comfortable platform for spine surgeries.</p></p>
