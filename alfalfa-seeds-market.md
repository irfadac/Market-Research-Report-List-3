<p><p>Alfalfa seeds are classified into two main market types based on their dormancy characteristics: dormant seeds and non-dormant seeds. Dormant seeds require a period of conditioning before they can germinate, while non-dormant seeds can germinate immediately after sowing. Dormant seeds are often preferred for long-term storage and transportation, as they have a longer shelf life. Non-dormant seeds, on the other hand, are favored for quick establishment in the field. Both types cater to different needs in the agriculture industry.</p></p>
