NB-IoT market specializes in narrowband IoT technology, providing low power consumption and cost-effective solutions for IoT devices. LPWAN market, on the other hand, offers long-range connectivity with low power consumption, making it suitable for applications that require communication over longer distances. Each market type serves specific purposes and addresses unique requirements in the telecom industry.</p></p>
