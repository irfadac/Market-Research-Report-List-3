<p><p>Mass finishing machines are used for deburring, polishing, and surface finishing of large quantities of parts simultaneously. Tumble finishing involves placing parts, media, and compounds in a rotating drum to achieve the desired finish. Vibratory finishing uses a vibrating container filled with parts, media, and compounds to achieve similar results. Other types of mass finishing machines include centrifugal disc finishing, drag finishing, and spindle finishing. Each type offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the parts being processed.</p></p>
