<p><p>Compressor seals are crucial for preventing the leakage of gases or liquids in various industrial applications. There are two main types of compressor seals in the market: contact seals and non-contact seals. Contact seals are designed to maintain close physical contact with the sealing surface to prevent leakage, while non-contact seals rely on a small gap between the seal and the surface to create a barrier. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the efficiency and reliability of compressors in different industries.</p></p>
