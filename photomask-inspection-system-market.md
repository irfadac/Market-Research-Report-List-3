<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask Inspection System is a critical process in semiconductor manufacturing. Die to Die (DD) method compares the patterns on two identical photomasks, checking for defects or discrepancies. Die to Database (DB) method compares the photomask pattern to a digital reference database, ensuring accuracy. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and integrity of photomasks used in the production of semiconductor devices. These technologies help identify and eliminate defects early in the manufacturing process, improving overall production efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
