<p><p>A non vacuum blood collection tube is a type of blood collection device that does not contain any vacuum to draw blood into the tube. They are commonly used in situations where a vacuum may not be suitable, such as in certain pediatric or fragile patient populations.</p><p>The Non Vacuum Blood Collection Tube Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.00% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to an increasing demand for non vacuum blood collection tubes in various healthcare settings, such as hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic centers. These tubes offer advantages such as reduced risk of hemolysis, decreased risk of clot formation, and lower cost compared to vacuum tubes.</p><p>The latest trend in the non vacuum blood collection tube market is the development of tubes with specialized coatings or additives to enhance sample stability and preservation.
