<p><ul><li>Devices</li><li>Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fleet telematics system market is divided into two main types: devices and software. Fleet telematics devices are hardware units installed in vehicles to collect and transmit data such as location, speed, and engine diagnostics to a central system. Fleet telematics software is the platform that processes and analyzes the data collected by the devices, providing valuable insights and reports for fleet management. Together, devices and software play a crucial role in optimizing fleet operations, improving safety, and reducing costs.</p></p>
