<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Underfill Dispensers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Flow Underfill</li><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capillary flow underfill dispensers are used to fill gaps between components through capillary action. No flow underfill dispensers are dispensed before the components are assembled and create a bond during the curing process. Molded underfill dispensers are pre-dispensed and molded under the component during the molding process. Each type of dispenser serves different purposes and is used in various industries for electronic packaging and assembly to improve the reliability and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
