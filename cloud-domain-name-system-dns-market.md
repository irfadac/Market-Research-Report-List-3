<p><p>Private Cloud-based DNS refers to a DNS system that is operated and managed by a single organization for its internal use only. It offers greater control and security over domain name resolution within the organization's private cloud network. On the other hand, Public Cloud-based DNS is a DNS system that is hosted and managed by a third-party service provider for use by multiple organizations or the general public. It offers scalability and accessibility to a wide range of users outside of a single organization's network.</p></p>
