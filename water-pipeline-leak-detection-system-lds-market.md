<p><p>Water Pipeline Leak Detection System (LDS) market can be segmented into three types based on the monitoring approach. Non-continuously monitoring systems require periodic inspections. Continuously with external measurements involve sensors placed outside the pipeline to detect leaks. In contrast, continuously with internal measurements use sensors within the pipeline to monitor leaks in real-time. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to the diverse needs of water pipeline operators in efficiently detecting and addressing leaks.</p></p>
