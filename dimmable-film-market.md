<p><p>Dimmable film is available in two main types: self-adhesive and non-adhesive. Self-adhesive dimmable film comes with a sticky backing that allows for easy installation on various surfaces, making it a popular choice for DIY projects. On the other hand, non-adhesive dimmable film requires a separate adhesive for installation. This type offers more flexibility in terms of application options and can be easily removed and repositioned. Both types of dimmable film are widely used for privacy, decoration, and energy-saving purposes in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
