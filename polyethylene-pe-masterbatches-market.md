<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) masterbatches are additives used in the plastic industry to enhance the properties of polyethylene products. There are three main types of PE masterbatches: LDPE masterbatches, HDPE masterbatches, and LLDPE masterbatches. LDPE masterbatches are used for low-density polyethylene products, HDPE masterbatches for high-density polyethylene products, and LLDPE masterbatches for linear low-density polyethylene products. Each type of masterbatch offers specific benefits and properties, making them suitable for different applications in the plastic industry.</p></p>
