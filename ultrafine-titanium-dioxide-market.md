<p><strong>The Ultrafine Titanium Dioxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatase Based</li><li>Rutile Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrafine Titanium Dioxide market can be classified into two types based on the crystal structure of the nanoparticles: Anatase based and Rutile based. Anatase based titanium dioxide particles are known for their higher reactivity and surface area, making them suitable for applications requiring photocatalytic properties. On the other hand, Rutile based titanium dioxide particles are valued for their higher stability and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring superior UV resistance and opacity. Both types offer unique advantages and are utilized in various industries.</p></p>
