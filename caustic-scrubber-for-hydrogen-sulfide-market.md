<p><p>Caustic scrubbers for hydrogen sulfide removal are commonly found in two main types: vertical scrubbers (also known as counter-flow scrubbers) and horizontal scrubbers (also known as cross-flow scrubbers). Vertical scrubbers allow for the gas and liquid to flow in opposite directions, maximizing contact between the two phases. Horizontal scrubbers have gas and liquid flowing perpendicular to each other, allowing for efficient removal of hydrogen sulfide. Both types of scrubbers are effective in removing hydrogen sulfide from gas streams in various industrial applications.</p></p>
