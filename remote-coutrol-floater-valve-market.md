<p><p>Remote control floater valves come in three main types - through type, three way type, and right angle type. Through type valves allow flow straight through the valve, while three way type valves have three openings for more complex flow control. Right angle valves are designed to change the direction of flow at a right angle. Each type is used in different applications depending on the desired flow control and direction requirements.</p></p>
