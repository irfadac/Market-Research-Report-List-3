<p><p>Powder Bed Fusion is a type of 3D printing technology used for producing parts and products. Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) uses a high-powered laser to fuse metal powder together. Selective Heat Sintering (SHS) employs an infrared lamp to selectively heat and fuse powder. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses a laser to sinter powdered material layer by layer. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) utilizes an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powder. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) involves melting metal powder with a high-powered laser.</p></p>
