<p><p>Epoxy and polyurethane are two major types of automotive structural adhesives used in the market. Epoxy adhesives are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for bonding metal components in vehicles. On the other hand, polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to vibrations, making them suitable for bonding materials with different coefficients of thermal expansion. Both adhesives play a crucial role in enhancing the structural integrity of automobiles, improving overall safety and performance.</p></p>
