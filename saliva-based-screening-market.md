<p><p>Saliva-based screening involves different types of markets such as Direct Sample to PCR, RT-qPCR, and Lateral Flow Assays. Direct Sample to PCR market involves taking a saliva sample and directly analyzing it using Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) technology. RT-qPCR market utilizes reverse transcription before PCR to detect RNA molecules in saliva samples. Lateral Flow Assays market involves using a simple paper-based test strip to detect specific molecules in saliva, providing quick and easy screening results. These markets offer effective and convenient methods for saliva-based screening.</p></p>
