<p><p>The high-speed coupling market consists of various types of couplings designed for specific applications. Flexible elastic couplings allow for misalignment and absorb shock and vibration. Torsionally-rigid flexible couplings provide high torque transmission while accommodating slight misalignments. Rigid couplings offer direct power transmission with no flexibility, ideal for applications with precise alignment requirements. Flange couplings connect shafts with bolted flanges, providing a strong and secure connection. Each type of coupling caters to different needs in high-speed applications.</p></p>
