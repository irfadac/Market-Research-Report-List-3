<p><p>Surgical smart glasses are divided into two types: Wire Type and Wireless Type. Wire Type smart glasses are connected to a power source or data input device through a physical wire, providing a stable connection but limiting mobility. In contrast, Wireless Type smart glasses operate without the need for physical wires, offering greater freedom of movement for surgeons during procedures. Both types of smart glasses are designed to enhance surgical precision and efficiency by providing real-time information and visual guidance to the user.</p></p>
