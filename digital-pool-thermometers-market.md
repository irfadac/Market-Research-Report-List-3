<p><p>There are two main types of digital pool thermometers on the market: Cable Probe Type and Wireless Floating Type. Cable Probe Type thermometers have a probe that is connected to a display unit outside of the pool, allowing for accurate temperature readings without needing to be in the water. Wireless Floating Type thermometers float on the surface of the pool and transmit temperature data wirelessly to a separate display unit. Both types offer convenience and accuracy in monitoring pool temperature for optimal comfort and maintenance.</p></p>
