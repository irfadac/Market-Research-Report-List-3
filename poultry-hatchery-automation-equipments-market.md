<p><p>Poultry hatchery automation equipment market includes various types of incubators such as walk-in incubators and tunnel type incubators. Walk-in incubators are large rooms where eggs are placed on trays and manually turned, while tunnel type incubators have a conveyor system that moves eggs through a series of compartments with automated turning mechanisms. Both types of incubators provide controlled environments for optimal development of poultry embryos, ensuring efficient hatching rates and overall productivity in poultry hatcheries.</p></p>
