<p><ul><li>Plate Type</li><li>Tubular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PECVD equipment can be categorized into two main types: plate type and tubular type. Plate type PECVD equipment consists of multiple plates where the substrate material is placed for coating. This type is commonly used for large-area thin film deposition. On the other hand, tubular type PECVD equipment utilizes a tubular chamber where the substrate material is passed through for coating. This type is suitable for continuous process applications and is often used for coating long and narrow materials.</p></p>
