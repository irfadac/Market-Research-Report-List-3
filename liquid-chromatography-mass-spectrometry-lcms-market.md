There are different types of LCMS systems available, including Single Quadrupole LC-MS, Triple Quadrupole LC-MS, and Ion Trap LC-MS. Single Quadrupole LC-MS is commonly used for qualitative analysis, while Triple Quadrupole LC-MS is used for quantitative analysis due to its high sensitivity and selectivity. Ion Trap LC-MS offers increased flexibility and higher sensitivity, making it suitable for a wide range of applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and more.</p></p>
