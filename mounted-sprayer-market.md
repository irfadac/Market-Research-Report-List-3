<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mounted Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-point Hitch</li><li>ATV-mounted</li><li>Front-mount</li><li>Straddle Tractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mounted sprayers are commonly categorized based on their mounting types. The 3-point hitch mounted sprayers are connected to the tractor via a 3-point hitch system, providing stability and easy maneuverability. ATV-mounted sprayers are mounted on all-terrain vehicles for versatility in rugged terrains. Front-mount sprayers are mounted on the front of the tractor for better visibility and control. Straddle tractor sprayers are mounted between the front and rear axles of the tractor, allowing for better weight distribution and maneuverability. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
