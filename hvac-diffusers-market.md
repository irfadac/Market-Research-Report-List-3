Ceiling mounted diffusers are commonly used in commercial buildings to distribute air evenly throughout a room. Wall mounted diffusers are often seen in residential settings and provide targeted air flow in specific areas. Floor mounted diffusers are typically used in spaces where air circulation near the floor is necessary, such as in industrial buildings or warehouses. Each type serves a specific purpose in improving indoor air quality and comfort.</p></p>
