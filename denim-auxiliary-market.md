<p><p>The denim auxiliary market includes various types such as thickener, binding agent, cross linking agent, emulsifier, fixing agent, dispersing agent, defoamers, and others. Thickener is used to increase the viscosity of denim dye solutions, while binding agents help in binding dye particles to the fabric. Cross linking agents are used to improve fabric strength and durability. Emulsifiers aid in dispersing dye evenly in the solution. Fixing agents help in fixing the dye on the fabric while dispersing agents ensure uniform dye distribution. Defoamers are used to remove foam during the dyeing process.</p></p>
