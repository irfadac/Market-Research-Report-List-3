<p><p>Batch coding machines are used in industries to print information like expiration dates, lot numbers, and barcodes on products. Contact coding machines come in direct contact with the product's surface, using techniques like ink jet or hot stamping. Non-contact coding machines use technologies like laser or thermal transfer to mark products without touching them. Both types of machines are essential for ensuring accurate and efficient product labeling, with each offering unique advantages depending on the application and industry requirements.</p></p>
