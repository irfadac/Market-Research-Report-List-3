<p><p>Polyester adhesives market types include solvents, hardening initiators, hardening catalysts, and extenders. Solvents help dissolve the adhesive for application. Hardening initiators trigger the curing process, while hardening catalysts accelerate it. Extenders are used to modify the properties of the adhesive, such as improving flexibility or increasing strength. Each type plays a vital role in the performance and characteristics of polyester adhesives, catering to various applications and industries.</p></p>
