<p><p>Exercise resistance bands come in two main types: strip bands and tube bands. Strip bands are flat, wide bands that typically have handles on each end, making them ideal for exercises that require a secure grip, such as arm curls or rows. Tube bands, on the other hand, are circular bands with handles on both ends, allowing for a wider range of motion and versatility in exercises like squats, chest presses, and lunges. Both types offer varying levels of resistance to accommodate different fitness levels and workout intensities.</p></p>
