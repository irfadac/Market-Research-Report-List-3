<p><p>Desalination pumps are essential in the process of removing salt and impurities from seawater to make it drinkable. There are two main types of pumps used in desalination plants: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotating impellers to increase the pressure of the water, making it suitable for the desalination process. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, use a mechanism to displace a specific volume of water with each cycle, providing a consistent flow for the desalination process. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and effective desalination operations.</p></p>
