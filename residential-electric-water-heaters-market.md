<p><p>Residential electric water heaters come in two main types: tankless electric water heaters and tank electric water heaters. Tankless electric water heaters heat water on-demand, providing a continuous supply of hot water without the need for a storage tank. On the other hand, tank electric water heaters store hot water in a tank for future use, allowing for a larger reservoir of hot water. Both types of water heaters have their own advantages and are suitable for different household needs.</p></p>
