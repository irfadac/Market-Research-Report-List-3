<p><p>Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometers (RTD) are sensors used for measuring temperature in various industrial applications. <p><p>Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometers (RTD) are temperature sensors that use a metal conductor wrapped in mineral insulation material. In the simplex conductor market, a single metal conductor is used, while in the duplex conductor market, two metal conductors are utilized for improved accuracy and redundancy. Simplex conductors are cost-effective and suitable for basic temperature measurement applications, while duplex conductors offer higher reliability and precision, making them ideal for critical temperature monitoring in industries such as manufacturing and HVAC.</p></p>
