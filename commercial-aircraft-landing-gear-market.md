<p><ul><li>Strut Landing Gear</li><li>Rocker Landing Gear</li><li>Pontoon Landing Gear</li><li>Framed Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Aircraft Landing Gear Market includes four main types: Strut Landing Gear, Rocker Landing Gear, Pontoon Landing Gear, and Framed Landing Gear. Strut Landing Gear is the most common type, featuring a single strut extending from the wing to the bottom of the aircraft. Rocker Landing Gear, on the other hand, is similar to strut landing gear but includes a shock-absorbing mechanism. Pontoon Landing Gear is used on seaplanes and consists of floats for water landings, while Framed Landing Gear involves a complex framework for support and stability during landing.</p></p>
