<p><ul><li>2D AOI</li><li>3D AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) technology has two main market types: 2D AOI and 3D AOI. 2D AOI systems inspect PCBs for defects using cameras and lighting to capture 2D images for analysis. On the other hand, 3D AOI systems use advanced technology to capture height information along with 2D images, allowing for more accurate detection of defects such as lifted leads or tombstoning. Both market types cater to the electronics manufacturing industry, providing quality control and efficiency in PCB production processes.</p></p>
