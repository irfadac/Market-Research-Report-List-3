<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices are used to treat heart failure by coordinating the contractions of the heart's chambers. There are two main types of CRT devices: Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers (CRT-P) and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-D). CRT-P devices help control the heart rate, while CRT-D devices not only regulate the heart rate but also deliver electrical shocks if a dangerous heart rhythm is detected. These devices have significantly improved the quality of life and outcomes for patients with heart failure.</p></p>
