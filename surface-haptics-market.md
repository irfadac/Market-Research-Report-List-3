<p><p>Surface haptics technology is categorized into three main types: Electrotactile Technologies, which use low-voltage electrical signals to create tactile feedback on surfaces; Thermal Technologies, which generate sensations of heat or coolness through the use of thermoelectric materials; and Mechanical Feedback Technologies, which utilize physical vibrations or movements to provide tactile sensations. These technologies aim to enhance user experiences in various applications such as virtual reality, gaming, and touchscreens by providing realistic and immersive haptic feedback.</p></p>
