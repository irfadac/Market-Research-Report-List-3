<p><p>Electronic Grade Fiberglass Yarn is classified into three main types based on diameter: Thick (8-9 microns), Thin (5-7 microns), and Ultrathin (Below 5 microns). Thick yarn is known for its durability and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring robustness. Thin yarn offers flexibility and resistance to high temperatures, making it ideal for use in various electronic components. Ultrathin yarn, with its extremely fine diameter, is valued for its exceptional insulation properties and is commonly used in delicate electronic devices.</p></p>
