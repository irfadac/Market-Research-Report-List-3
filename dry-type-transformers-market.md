<p><p>Dry-type transformers are categorized into two main market types: Single-Phase and Three-Phase. On the other hand, Three-Phase transformers are commonly employed in industrial and commercial applications where higher power capacities are required, operating at voltage levels of 208Y/120, 480Y/277, and 600Y/347 volts. Both types play essential roles in the efficient distribution of electrical power in different sectors.</p></p>
