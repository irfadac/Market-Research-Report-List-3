<p><strong>The Cosmetics Laminate Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetics laminate tube packaging market types include ABL (aluminum barrier laminate) and PBL (plastic barrier laminate) tubes. ABL tubes are made of multiple layers, including a layer of aluminum, to provide high barrier properties against oxygen and light. PBL tubes, on the other hand, are made entirely of plastic and offer a more flexible and lightweight option. Both ABL and PBL tubes are widely used in the cosmetics industry for products such as creams, lotions, and gels.</p></p>
