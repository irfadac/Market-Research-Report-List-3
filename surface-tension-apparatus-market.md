<p><p>Surface tension apparatus market can be divided into two main types: static and dynamic surface tension test equipment. Static surface tension test equipment measures the surface tension of a liquid while it is at rest, providing information about the liquid's ability to resist external forces. Dynamic surface tension test equipment, on the other hand, measures the surface tension while the liquid is in motion, offering insights into how the liquid behaves in dynamic conditions such as spraying or foaming. Both types of equipment serve important roles in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
