<p><p>ICU Ventilator market can be categorized into two main types: Invasive and Non-Invasive ventilators. </p><p>Invasive ventilators are used for patients who are unable to breathe on their own and require support through an endotracheal tube or tracheostomy. These ventilators provide mechanical ventilation by delivering air directly into the lungs.</p><p>Non-invasive ventilators, on the other hand, deliver oxygen to the patient's lungs without the need for an invasive procedure. This type of ventilator is often used for patients with less severe respiratory issues or for those who cannot tolerate invasive ventilation.</p></p>
